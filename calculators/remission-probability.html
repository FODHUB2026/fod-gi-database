How This Works

This calculator is based on outcomes from the DiRECT trial (Diabetes Remission Clinical Trial), which found that ~86% of participants who lost 15+ kg within 6 years of diagnosis achieved remission. Adjustments are applied for individual factors:

Base: 86% (if <6 years, >=15 kg loss, A1c <=7%, age <=65, BMI <=35) Duration adj: -10% per year over 6 (max -50%) Weight loss adj: -15% per 5 kg below 15 kg target A1c adj: -5% per 0.5% above 7.0% (max -30%) Age adj: -10% if age >65 BMI adj: +5% if BMI >=35 (more fat mass to lose = more potential)

Remission is defined as HbA1c below 6.5% without glucose-lowering medications for at least 3 months. This calculator applies to type 2 diabetes only - it is not applicable to type 1 or LADA. Even a low probability does not mean remission is impossible - every individual's response differs.
